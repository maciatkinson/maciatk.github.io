Mental Health Effects

Social media can have both positive and negative effects on mental health. Many people use social platforms to stay connected with friends and family or to find supportive communities.

However, excessive use of social media has been linked to higher levels of anxiety, stress, and depression among young adults. Constant exposure to curated images of other people's lives can also create unrealistic expectations.

Researchers continue to study how social media habits affect overall well-being and emotional health.
